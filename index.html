About

Who is Seth Abstract?

I create experimental music built from classic poetry, translating timeless language into modern sound.

Each project explores a different emotional and sonic identity, combining visual art, storytelling, and production into a unified experience.

This is less about genre and more about interpretation—what happens when old words meet new forms.
